Nelson is the prototypical point forward, who can impact the game in many different ways. At 6'11, he has the size and athleticism to overpower people around the rim, as well as defend the paint with his length. Nelson also has unique guard skills, and is very comfortable handling the ball on the perimeter. Defensively, Grant can guard multiple positions, though he can struggle against bigger, stronger centers. Nelson has been consistent with improving his three point shot, as well as his decision making, and is arguably the most versatile player in college basketball.
